    📐 Key concepts & formulae cheat-sheet

    Supplements lecture PDFs — verify equations against your adopted code edition.

    • Risk layering: passive fire (barriers/compartmentation) + active fire (detection/suppression) + egress + operator training.
    • BIM/coord clash matrix: structure/MEP shafts vs cable tray bends vs rated walls — track in weekly coordination logs.
    • O&M + as-builts: correlate jack labels ⇄ patching records ⇄ test reports for traceability.
    • CCTV/optical budget: consider insertion loss budgets for fiber/coax cascades vs receiver sensitivity margins.
    • ACS & life safety interfaces: fail-safe egress often requires FACP relays cutting power locks — confirm sequence of operation.
    • EMI control: maintain separation/power harmonics grounding for low-noise ancillary circuits.
    • Characteristic impedance: modern balanced twisted pair ≈100 Ω; maintain consistent terminations.
    • Fiber budgeting: total loss Σ(αfiber·L + αsplice·Nsplice + αconn·Nconn + splitter) must be ≤ available margin vs Tx/Rx curves.
    • Bandwidth × distance: upgrade category or move to OM4/SMF when ethernet speed climbs at fixed reach.
    • Classic channel budgeting (training shorthand): ≤≈90 m horizontal solid + ≤≈10 m patching → ≤100 m copper channel depending on gauges & de-ratings (consult adopted TIA edition).
    • 10GBASE‑T caveat: Cat6 reaches ~55 m channels for 10 G in many catalogs; Cat6A targets full 100 m channels with tighter alien crosstalk control.
    • Permanent link vs channel: field certify installed fixed segment separately from ephemeral patch cords swapping at MAC work.
    • Occupant load reductions: egress width computations depend on code tables + exit remoteness assumptions — always annotate calculation sheets.
    • Smoke control: stair pressurization / zone extraction interact with HVAC — integrated testing required.
    • Dated decree: PD 1096 issued 19 Feb 1977, superseding RA 6541 framework per official compilations.
    • NBC IRR issued through DPWH — confirm current memoranda for newest amendments.
    • Signed repealer: RA 9514 approved 19 Dec 2008, replaces PD 1185 Fire Code lineage.
    • Enforcement arcs through DILG / BFP IRR — fees/FSEC/FSIC workflows per latest circulars.
    • Alarm + ECS scope: mass notification overlays share infrastructure discipline with audible/visible alerting.
